# customer_Sales_satisfaction
This projects analyze customer sales satisfaction to identify customersegment tiering,Satisfaction Quartile,average sales by segment etc.The goal is to provide insights that can help improve business decisions.

--

## Business Problem
The company wants to understand:
* what is the average custumer sales by segment?
* How many percentage of customer are satisfied?
* What is the rank of custumer in their segemet?

  --

  ## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle
- Records: 100+
- Features:
  Group
  Customer_Segment
  Sales_Before
  Sales_After
  Customer_Satisfaction_Before
  Customer_Satisfaction_After
  Purchase_Made
  Sales_Growth
  Satisfaction_Growth
  Engagement_Score
  ROI_index
